Normality Test

Null hypothesis (H0): The samples follow a normal distribution.

Power comparison:

  • Shapiro-Wilk: The best of the three methods, especially for samples of less than 30 elements.

  • D'Agostino-Pearson: It's less powerful than Shapiro-Wilk.

  • Kolmogorov-Smirnov: The less powerful.
