ANOVA test and Bonferroni test [?]

ANOVA:

  • Null hypothesis (H0): The means of the results of two or more algorithms are the same.
  • It should comply: normality, homocedasticity and independence.

Bonferroni:

  • Null hypothesis (H0): The mean of the results of the "Algorithm A" and "Algorithm B" is equal (compares all algorithms each other).
  • Is applied if: ANOVA indicates that there are differences.
